# Electrophysiology-Helper
This repository has methods for plotting Cell Attached (Voltage Clamp), Whole Cell (both Voltage Clamp, and Current Clamp), IH (both Voltage Clamp, and Current Clamp)
This was made in collaboration at Northwestern University under Dr. D. James Surmeier the Nathan Smith Davis Professor and Chair of the Department of Neuroscience at the Feinberg School of Medicine.
